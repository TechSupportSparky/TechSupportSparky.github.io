The texture manager provide a way for you to load textures into your games. You can either load in simple color data defined by R\+G\+BA values, or a custom texture formatted in the T\+A\+R\+GA R\+AW (Truevision T\+GA) format A\+KA .tga. These textures are loaded in first with a \char`\"{}key\char`\"{} value to be referred to later and their associated texture.
